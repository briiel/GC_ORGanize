Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions.

Who can log in?

Students, organization officers, and admins can sign in using official school credentials.

How do I get my e‑certificate?

After attending and the event is concluded, go to E‑certificate in the app and download your certificate. Note: e‑certificates are issued for OSWS events only.

Are e‑certificates available for all events?

No. E‑certificates are available for OSWS events only. Organization‑hosted events may not provide e‑certificates through this system.

Do I need to install a mobile app?

No. GC‑ORGANIZE is a PWA—add it to your home screen from your browser for an app‑like experience.

What if I can’t scan the QR?

Ask an event marshal to assist or use your student ID to check in.
